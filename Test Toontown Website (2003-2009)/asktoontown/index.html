Dear Toontown,
I have a gold rod so why does it take so long to catch all 70 fish species? Do you have to catch a certain number in each "Fish Family" before a new one can be found?
Lisa, St. Joseph, MO
Dear Lisa,
The number of species that you catch in each "Fish Family" will not affect your ability to catch all 70 species. Your gold rod will allow you to catch some of the heavier fish species, though, and should help on your mission to find all 70! Be advised that there are several Ultra Rare fish, including the Full Moon Fish, Devil Ray, and Concord Peanut Butter & Jellyfish. The more you fish, the better the odds that you'll find them! Just keep looking in all possible ponds in the different neighborhoods and try fishing for the All-Star Fish and Holey Mackerel at your Toon's Estate. Check out the Toontown Players' Guide for more helpful tips and information on fishing!
Happy Fishing,
Your Friends At Toontown
Dear Toontown,
My friend went to a store in The Brrrgh and turned all white. When I asked her what store or even street she couldn't remember. How can I make my Toon look like this?
Alexis
Dear Alexis,
Turn your Toon polar white by following these simple steps. First, visit the shopkeeper Paula Behr on the street Polar Place. Paula can be found in the Hibernation Vacations shop. Once inside, say the speedchat phrase "Howdy" near Paula and your Toon will turn white for exactly 1 hour. Remember, this special arctic appearance will only last while your Toon is in Polar Place.
Stay Chilly,
Your Friends at Toontown
Dear Toontown,
I started gardening at my Toon's Estate but I'm confused about how you sell the flowers. Where do you do this?
Angel R.
Dear Angel,
In order to sell a flower, first walk over to it and select "Pick." Once you pick the flower, it will appear in your basket which can hold up to 20 flowers. Walk over to the blue wheelbarrow behind your Estate in the brown sandy area. Your flower basket will appear, and you'll be asked if you'd like to sell all your flowers. Click "Sell All," and you'll receive one jellybean and one shovel skill point per flower!
Good luck gardening,
Your Friends at Toontown
Dear Toontown,
I don't know how to go in the doors to the Sellbot V.P. How do I get through?
Cody
Dear Cody,
Before you can face the Sellbot V.P., you must assemble your Cog Disguise. To do this, you need to enter the Sellbot Factory. Each time you defeat the Factory Foreman at the top of the central silo, you earn one piece of your Toon's Cog Disguise. The disguise has ten parts - that means you need to defeat the Factory Foreman ten times to complete the disguise. Once you’ve completed your Cog Disguise and have earned enough Sellbot merits to be ready for a promotion, the doors to Sellbot Towers will open for you. Your Toon will automatically put on his or her Cog disguise as you walk through the door.
Good luck gardening,
Your Friends at Toontown
