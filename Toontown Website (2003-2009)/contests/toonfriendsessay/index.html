A big thank you to all of the Toons who submitted essays for the Toon Friends Essay Contest! Join us in congratulating the top four Toontastic winning entries -- each has won a Disney 2.4Ghz Cordless Phone!

Jacob, S., 8
My Toontastic friend is Silly Ned, who actually is my best friend from school. He is really Toontastic because we help each other beat Cogs and with ToonTasks. We also like buying special things for each other from the Cattlelogs. I like opening my Toontown mailbox and finding something special that was bought for my Toon, B.D. Nicklewolf from Silly Ned. We play online together and no matter how hard the ToonTasks, we will always do it together. We race on Goofy Speedway and play Trolley games. I really think it is Toontastic having my best friend on Toontown.

Hope, B., 13
She is a bear and I am a rabbit, who cares if yelling "Owoooo!" is a really bad habit.
She's so cool and lots of fun, when we're fighting Cogs, she'll never run.
We stand up for each other, complete ToonTasks, too!
Yes, we do this because this is what sisters and best friends do!
When our Laff Meters are low and we're feeling quite sad, our Doodles will come to save the day!
"Hooray for Sugarbaby and Olympia," we'll say.
Sometimes we fight and sometimes we bicker and often we disagree about who is quicker.
But I love her so much, yes I truly do. "Sissy, I would defeat all the Cogs in Toontown for you!!!"
I love my best Toon friend, and sister, Princess Bonnie!!!

Rachel, B., 10
If I were to say who my best friend in Toontown was, I'd say her name is Ugly Scooter Wrinkleface. The name may sound bad to you, but it is the prettiest name I know. She is taller than me. But I say she needs her tallness for her heart. She's pink and has much more Laff points than me, but that's all right. She’s probably my only friend left from when I was a kitten. When I get a new Gag, or she has a better Gag than me, we say "Nice gag!" When one of us is busy, and the other needs assistance, we wait patiently for each other. Cogs to buildings to Headquarters -- we've done it all. When I log-in Princess Candy, I always make sure Ugly Scooter Wrinkleface is still on my Friends List. And she always is.

Tiffany, C., 11
Sally Flipperbubble is my bestest, best friend. She is always sending me gifts from Clarabelle's Cattlelog, and whenever I need help, she is always there. She and I are always together and she's always asking me, "Where should we go?" Sometimes we say the same thing at the same time. Then we both giggle. She usually helps me with my ToonTasks and I help her with hers. Once we mistakenly went into a Cog building. We both got sad and Sally apologized. I told her that it wasn't her fault. Sally truly is a stupendous, awesome, absolutely wonderful, amazing friend.
